Our story

Named for a field of red.

Every summer, wild red poppies light up the hills around Alpine, Utah. Poppy Crêpes takes its name, its color, and its cheerful spirit from them — a little pop of red at the foot of the mountains.

We fold French-style crêpes on the griddle, sweet and savory, alongside espresso, tea, and a freezer full of ice cream and gelato. Modern, playful, and made to order — come say hi.

We’re women-owned and Alpine-grown, too. Poppy started when a local — born and raised right here — traded a career in AI and big tech for a griddle and a French crêpe pan, and brought the craft back home to the mountains.
